What do you think of this story?

This is a web-based experiment that asks people to read stories and answer simple questions. Before taking part in this study, please read the consent form below and click on the "I Agree" button at the bottom of the page to indicate that you understand the statements and freely consent to participate in the study.

Participation in the study takes between 5 and 10 minutes. Participants begin by answering a series of control questions, after which they will be asked to read a story. This story may involve moderate graphic content such as that found in newspaper articles. Participants will then be asked to rate what they thought of the story through a visual scale. Afterwards, participants are asked some additional questions for verification purposes.

You will be asked to provide your Worker Id, to ensure that you only complete the experiment once. All responses are treated as confidential and your responses will not be linked to your identity. Be aware that confidentiality will be maintained to the extent possible. There is always the risk of compromising privacy, confidentiality and/or anonymity when using email and the internet. However, the risk to your physical, emotional, social, professional or financial well-being is considered to be minimal.

Your participation is voluntary, and you will be compensated for completing the experiment once. Refusal to take part in the study involves no penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled, and you may withdraw from the study at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.
